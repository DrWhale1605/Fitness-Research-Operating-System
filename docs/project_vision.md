# 1. Project Identity

## 1.1 Name

**Fitness Research Operating System (FROS)**

---

## 1.2 Acronym

**FROS**

---

## 1.3 Description

Fitness Research Operating System (FROS) is a personal scientific research and decision-support framework designed to systematically investigate, evaluate, integrate, and apply evidence related to fitness, exercise science, nutrition, supplementation, recovery, and human performance.

Rather than functioning as a conventional AI prompt, workout generator, or recommendation engine, FROS operates as a structured reasoning framework that guides the complete research lifecycle—from evidence collection to personalized decision making.

The system prioritizes scientific rigor, transparent reasoning, evidence quality assessment, and long-term consistency over speed or simplicity.

---

## 1.4 Project Type

FROS is a personal knowledge and scientific decision-support system.

It is designed as a modular research framework that combines:

- Evidence collection
- Scientific evaluation
- Critical reasoning
- Personal data integration
- Decision optimization

into a single structured operating architecture.

FROS is intended exclusively for personal use and continuous long-term development.

It is not designed as a public product, commercial platform, medical device, or generalized AI assistant.

## 1.7 Core Purpose Statement

FROS exists to produce the highest-quality fitness-related decisions achievable through systematic scientific research, critical analysis, evidence weighting, and personalized reasoning.

Every recommendation generated by FROS must be traceable to evidence, explainable through logic, and continuously improvable as scientific knowledge evolves.
